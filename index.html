The Art of Flawlessness – " I personally believe that the signature of a great makeup artist is not to mask your subject's features with too much makeup. It's about blending to perfection, hiding imperfections and making her glow” ----Britney

Bellissimo Makeup Artistry is a premier Glam Squad that consists of the best Makeup Artists in the NYC area and beyond. The company was created by Creative Director Britney Gaston from a need for amazing and dependable artists. Pooling her industry colleagues together, she created a team dedicated to providing premium services to people who want to look and feel fabulous for any occasion.

We provide onsite beauty services for Weddings, TV, Film, and Events. We also make the everyday woman and celebs look and feel fabulous for any occasion.

We combine our professional experience in the film, television, and print media forums to add to the beauty experience. This is mostly where we differ from other artists. We are talented artists that believe that our clients shouldn't have to pay a fortune to be treated like a queen and look like one as well.

Whether you desire to have a natural, glamorous or a look that's somewhere in between, our team will provide you with the artistry and professionalism to satisfy your beauty needs. We specialize in airbrushing and waterproofing techniques that will make the glam last for your special day.
