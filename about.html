About Britney and Bellissimo Makeup


My name is Britney Gaston. I am a graduate of the New York Academy of Cosmetics Arts in Manhattan, New York. I participated and completed advance training in makeup for movies, modeling, and television. I fell in love with makeup at the age of 11. I would constantly take my older sister's makeup and create different looks. Once my sister started selling makeup for a major brand, I tagged along as an assistant to her makeup parties. I've worked as a beauty consultant at a makeup counter for two years, which enabled me to practice every makeup technique daily on customers. I've spent most of my adult life learning about techniques, products, trends, tools, and the history of makeup.

My passion for makeup has opened doors to several opportunities and I am so grateful for each and every one of them. I have worked with celebrities including Rihanna and Katy Perry, most currently doing makeup at New York Fashion week. After building up my skills and clientele I decided it was time to start my own freelance business, Bellissimo Makeup Artistry. I broke into the industry and fell in love with everything about it. There is nothing more special then being able to be a part of your clients big day.
